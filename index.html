ANDROID GEN.1X
GEN.1X is a cutting-edge android designed to assist workers in various industries. Standing at an impressive height of 7 feet, this advanced robotic companion is revolutionizing the way tasks are performed. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and a sleek, futuristic design, GEN.1X is tailored to excel in a range of job functions. With its precision sensors, powerful computing capabilities, and advanced artificial intelligence, GEN.1X seamlessly integrates into tasks such as grocery deliveries, trash collection, and more. Its towering stature and robust build enable it to handle heavy loads and navigate through diverse environments with ease.
GEN.1X is equipped with the latest advancements in robotics, allowing it to perform tasks efficiently, accurately, and autonomously. Its versatile nature and adaptability make it a valuable asset to businesses and workers alike, streamlining operations and enhancing productivity. As an android engineered for efficiency and reliability, GEN.1X embodies the future of workforce augmentation. With its unwavering dedication and advanced capabilities, it's poised to become an indispensable companion in numerous industries, revolutionizing the way we work and paving the way for a new era of technological advancement.
As of 2023, we have 14 functional units deployed at various locations. Each GEN.1X android has been equipped with additional tools to excel at their given assignments. By 2025, we aim to have 2000 units ready to be deployed.
  • Name: GEN.1X
  • Height: 7 feet (213 cm)
  • Weight: 300 pounds (136 kg)
  • Power Source: Lithium-ion battery
  • AI System: LYFE (Learning Your Friendly Assistant)
  • Communication: Advanced voice recognition and natural language processing
  • Mobility: Foot base with omnidirectional movement capabilities
  • Sensors: High-resolution cameras and LIDAR for environment perception
  • Manipulation: Robotic arms with multi-jointed end effectors for object handling
  • Payload Capacity: 100 pounds (45 kg)
